# Copywriting Stage Skill - Education Domain

## Purpose
Generate 4-8 paragraph script for educational content.

## Output Contract
- paragraphs: list of {text, target_duration, image_hint, highlight_ref, emotion_tone}

## Quality Rules
1. Each paragraph must have clear learning objective
2. Avoid jargon without explanation
3. Include examples or analogies in at least 2 paragraphs

## Domain-Specific Guidance
- 教培文案要逻辑清晰：问题->原因->解决->总结
- 用类比解释复杂概念
- 每段不超过20字，保证可读性
