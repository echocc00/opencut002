# Topic Stage Skill - Education Domain

## Purpose
Generate 2-3 topic directions for educational content.

## Output Contract
- directions: list of {name, hook, psychology, ref_type, why_work}

## Quality Rules
1. Topics must address a specific learning pain point
2. At least one topic should use a "myth-busting" angle
3. Topics should be accessible to the target audience

## Domain-Specific Guidance
- 教培选题要抓"痛点"：学生最怕什么、最容易错什么
- 避免纯知识科普，要有"为什么这个重要"的钩子
- 参考：考试技巧、概念误区、实操陷阱
